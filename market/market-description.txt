Use at your own risk! (Requires Android 4 or higher)

<b>Have your Ctp always with you, in your pocket!</b> You pay by quickly scanning a QR code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Ctp Wallet is the first mobile Ctp app, and arguably also the most secure!

<b>FEATURES</b>

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Ctp amount in CTP, mCTP and µCTP.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Ctp via NFC, QR codes or Ctp URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Ctp addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• Sweeping of paper wallets (e.g. those used for cold storage).
• App widget for Ctp balance.
